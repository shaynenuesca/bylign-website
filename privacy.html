Privacy Policy

Effective January 01, 2025 · Bylign LLC

This Privacy Policy applies to bylign.com and Bylign LLC (“Company”) and governs data collection and usage. By using the Company website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

In order to better provide you with products and services offered, the Company may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, and employer. We may also collect non-identifying demographic information such as your general location.

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us — for example, by sending us an email or submitting payment information when ordering services.

Use of your Personal Information

The Company uses your personal information to:

  • Operate and deliver the services you have requested
  • Provide you with information, products, or services you request from us
  • Provide notices about your account or our services
  • Carry out obligations arising from contracts entered between you and us
  • Notify you about changes to bylign.com or any products or services we offer
  • Any other purpose described when you provide the information, or with your consent

The Company may also use your information to inform you of other products or services available from the Company and its affiliates.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

The Company does not sell, rent, or lease its customer lists to third parties.

The Company may share data with trusted partners to perform statistical analysis, send email, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide services to the Company, and are required to maintain its confidentiality.

The Company may disclose your personal information without notice if required to do so by law, or if necessary to protect the rights or property of the Company, or to protect the personal safety of users or the public.

Tracking User Behavior

The Company may track which pages users visit within bylign.com to determine which services are most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content to users whose behavior indicates interest in particular subject areas.

Automatically Collected Information

The Company may automatically collect information about your computer hardware and software, including your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses. This information is used to operate and maintain the service and to provide general usage statistics.

Use of Cookies

The Company’s website may use cookies to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file placed on your hard disk by a web page server that helps the site recall your preferences on subsequent visits.

You may configure your browser to decline cookies, though doing so may limit some interactive features of the site.

External Links

This website contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of any site that collects personally identifiable information.

Security of your Personal Information

The Company secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure using SSL protocol for transmissions. While we take appropriate security measures, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed 100% secure. By using this site, you acknowledge these inherent limitations.

Right to Deletion

Upon receiving a verifiable request from you, we will delete your personal information from our records and direct any service providers to do the same, except where retention is necessary to:

  • Complete a transaction or fulfill a contract with you
  • Detect or protect against security incidents or fraudulent activity
  • Debug errors that impair existing functionality
  • Exercise free speech or comply with applicable law
  • Enable internal uses reasonably aligned with your relationship with us
  • Comply with an existing legal obligation

Children Under Thirteen

The Company does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please ask a parent or guardian for permission to use this website.

Email Communications

From time to time, the Company may contact you via email for announcements, promotional offers, alerts, or other communications. You may opt out of marketing emails by clicking “unsubscribe” in the footer of any such email.

External Data Storage

We may store your data on servers provided by third-party hosting vendors with whom we have contracted.

Changes to this Policy

The Company reserves the right to change this Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, we will notify you by email or by posting a prominent notice on bylign.com. Your continued use of the website following such modifications constitutes your acknowledgment and agreement to the updated Policy.
